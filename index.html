About Us

Trevi is the brainchild of Joline Song and was founded with two other partners, Gordon Hon, her husband and Janice Wan, her best friend. The cafe is modeled after Italy and France so that people can enjoy the European atmosphere in the comfort of the city of Kuching itself. The name Trevi Cafe was chosen by taking from the Trevi fountain in Rome which is at the crossroads of three streets and it is closely related to the three early founders of Trevi Cafe..

Events

Birthday Partys

Celebrating a birthday of someone who’s a huge fan of Italian food? Well, our answer is to head on over to Trevi Café at Level 1 & 2, Block 5, Saradise Edge, Lot 19844, 93350 Kuching, Sarawak. Ranked as the number one of Italian restaurant in Kuching, you’ll be indulging yourself with heavenly dishes like squid ink seafood spaghetti, Boston lobster fettuccine and soft shell crab linguine. Their portions are generous, so that’s all the more reason to bring your celebration to this much-loved restaurant with friends and family.

Night Partys
